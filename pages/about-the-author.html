About the Author

Hello there!

This is Aman Sinha. I love gaming more than I loved my undergraduate courses. I graduated in Electronics & Communication Engineering from Haldia Institute Of Technology in June 2019. Although I took ECE, my main interest lies in Programming & Software development in general. I have worked on different academic projects during my stay at college which boosted my technical and programming knowledge. Like I worked on an Arduino-based robotic vehicle in my sophomore year where we programmed it to run only on black & white path. Currently, I am brushing up my technical skills by contributing to an open-source project OwO which is a simple discord bot that makes your discord experience more fun. It contains mini-games like slots, lotteries, battling, hunting, and many more.

Apart from that, I love playing FPS games which are first-person shooter games. I have played many online tournaments conducted by e-sports YouTubers during my stay at college and won cash prizes. My personal favorite is CS-GO in which I am currently ranked as Master Guardian Elite.

I find myself as a quick learner and I consider that as my biggest strength. Thank you!
